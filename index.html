Extra Security

When friends or company executives join a Copay wallet, more than one person must sign every transaction. If your computer is compromised and your private keys are stolen, the bitcoins are still safe. This is in addition to state-of-the-art encrypted storage and communication.

Decentralized

You shouldnâ€™t have to depend on a central organization to use bitcoin. Copay has almost no dependence on servers, and the servers it does use are open-source and can be run by anybody. As web browsers become capable, eventually the servers stop being necessary altogether.

Advanced Technology

Copay is possible due to recent advances in web technology that allow your bitcoins to be stored locally and not on a server, and for the copayers to communicate peer-to-peer rather than through a server. We also use the most modern bitcoin protocols that allow you to backup your wallet once, and you never have to worry about backing it up again.

Open-Source

Copay is completely open-source and has been since day 1. We welcome other people to copy and give back to Copay. We developed Copay for internal use at BitPay, but we all win if everybody has secure bitcoins, which is best made possible with open-source.
